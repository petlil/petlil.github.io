About

Peter is a musician and visual artist based in Wellington, New Zealand. He studied composition and sound art at the New Zealand School of Music, followed by computer science at Victoria University of Wellington. His multi-faceted artworks explore the worlds of sound art, visual art, and code.

Peter's musical interests are broad. He makes electroacoustic ambient music and sings in choirs and operas. He studied classical saxophone, and now plays mostly his own roaring compositions on an old bass saxophone. He also composes for film and makes audio plugins and apps in his spare time.
